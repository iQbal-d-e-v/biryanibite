Awadhi Biryani – A Balance of Fragrance, Texture, and Taste

Awadhi Biryani balances fragrant basmati rice, tender meat, and Kolkata-style potato, slow-cooked using the traditional dum method. Mild, aromatic spices enhance natural flavors, creating a refined, light, and perfectly harmonious biryani experience.

The Legacy of Awadhi Biryani

Awadhi Biryani traces its roots to the royal kitchens of Lucknow, where subtle flavors, refined cooking techniques, and respect for ingredients shaped a biryani known for elegance, balance, and timeless culinary heritage.
